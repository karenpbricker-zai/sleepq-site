Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my sleep?

Simply open SleepQ before bed and tap "Start Sleep." The app will automatically track your sleep throughout the night. Tap "Wake Up" when you rise in the morning.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes. Your sleep data is stored securely on your device and is never shared with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Which devices are supported?

SleepQ is available for iPhone running iOS 16 or later.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Account > Delete Account within the app.
