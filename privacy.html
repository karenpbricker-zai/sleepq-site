Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Introduction

SleepQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

SleepQ collects the following types of information:

  • Sleep Data: Sleep duration, sleep quality metrics, and wake times that you record through the app.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version for compatibility and troubleshooting purposes.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how you interact with the app to help us improve the experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the SleepQ service
  • Generate personalized sleep insights and recommendations
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your sleep data is stored locally on your device. We use industry-standard security measures to protect any information transmitted to our servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

SleepQ may use third-party services for analytics purposes. These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your sleep data from the app

Children's Privacy

SleepQ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sleepq.app.
