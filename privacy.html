Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • Email address (if you create an account or contact us)
  • Feedback or support messages you send us

B. Health Data (Apple Health Integration)

With your explicit permission through Apple Health, SleepQ may access certain health-related data, including:

  • Sleep duration
  • Time in bed
  • Sleep stages (including REM and deep sleep)
  • Heart rate (if used for sleep analysis)
  • Other sleep-related metrics you authorize

SleepQ only accesses the specific data types you choose to share through Apple Health.

Health data:

  • Is used solely to provide sleep insights and coaching
  • Is not used for advertising
  • Is not sold to third parties
  • Is not shared with data brokers

You can revoke Apple Health permissions at any time through your device settings.

C. Automatically Collected Information

We may collect limited technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App version
  • Anonymous usage analytics
  • Crash diagnostics

This information helps us improve app stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Analyze your sleep patterns
  • Generate personalized sleep insights and reports
  • Improve our sleep scoring models
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Communicate important updates (if you opt in)

We do not sell your personal or health data.

3. How We Share Information

We may share limited information with:

  • Service providers that help operate our infrastructure (e.g., cloud hosting or analytics providers)
  • Legal authorities if required by law
  • A successor entity in the event of a merger or acquisition

We do not share health data for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal and health data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide the Service
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete your personal and associated health data within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

5. Your Rights

As a U.S.-based user, you may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for health data access

Please contact us at support@sleepq.app.

You may also revoke Apple Health data access directly through your device settings at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

SleepQ is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Analytics Services

We may use third-party analytics tools to understand general usage patterns and improve the app. These tools collect anonymized or aggregated technical data. Health data from Apple Health is not shared with analytics providers for advertising purposes.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud hosting
  • Restricted internal access to data

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. U.S. Operations

SleepQ operates and stores data within the United States. By using the Service, you consent to the processing of your data in the United States.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date and may provide notice within the app.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@sleepq.app.
