Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking my sleep?

To start tracking your sleep, allow SleepQ to access your Health data.This lets us analyze your sleep patterns and provide personalized insights.You can manage permissions anytime in Apple Health.

Is my data stored securely?

Yes — your data is handled with care and protected using secure, industry-standard safeguards. SleepQ only accesses the Health data you choose to share, and only to power your sleep insights and personalized experience.

Which devices are supported?

SleepQ is designed for iPhone and works with Apple Watch, Oura Ring, and other sleep trackers that sync with Apple Health.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from Settings > Account > Delete Account within the app.
